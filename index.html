Privacy Policy

NPTE Prep App — Physical Therapy Board Exam Preparation

Last updated: May 3, 2025

Overview

NPTE Prep App ("the App," "we," "our") is an iOS application designed to help physical therapy students prepare for the National Physical Therapy Examination (NPTE). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Information We Collect

1. Subscription and Purchase Data

When you subscribe through the App, Apple processes your payment and manages your subscription. We receive only the information Apple provides to confirm your subscription status (active, expired, or in trial). We do not collect or store your name, email address, credit card number, or any other billing information. All billing is handled exclusively by Apple under their Privacy Policy.

2. Study Performance Data (stored on-device only)

All study data generated within the App — including quiz answers, diagnostic results, flashcard ratings, readiness scores, streaks, and study plans — is stored exclusively on your device using iOS local storage (UserDefaults). This data is:

  • Never transmitted to our servers (we do not operate a backend server).
  • Not linked to your identity or Apple ID by us.
  • Not shared with any third party by us.
  • Included in your device's local encrypted backup if you use iCloud Backup or iTunes Backup, which is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

3. AI Tutor Messages (optional feature)

The App includes an optional AI Tutor powered by Anthropic's Claude API. This feature requires you to supply your own Anthropic API key, which is stored securely in your device's iOS Keychain and is never transmitted to us. If you use the AI Tutor:

  • Your messages and the question context are sent directly from your device to Anthropic's servers at api.anthropic.com.
  • This data is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • We do not receive, log, or store your AI Tutor conversations.
  • The AI Tutor is entirely optional. The App is fully functional without it.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials (there is no account system).
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or location data.
  • Crash logs or analytics events (no third-party analytics SDK is used).
  • Any data used for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Tracking

The App does not track you across other companies' apps or websites. We do not use Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework because there is nothing to request permission for.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party, except as described below:

  • Apple: Subscription and purchase transactions are handled by Apple.
  • Anthropic: Only if you choose to use the optional AI Tutor feature and enter your own API key.

Data Retention and Deletion

All study data lives on your device. You can delete it at any time by deleting the App from your device. There is no server-side data to request deletion of.

To cancel your subscription, go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iOS device.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by adults (physical therapy graduate students and licensed clinicians). We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
